under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 only** — see [`LICENSE`](LICENSE).

You may use, study, modify, and self-host it. Because it is **AGPL**, if you run a
modified version as a network service, you must make your modified source
available to that service's users. This keeps the core open even in the cloud.

A separate **commercial license** is available for organizations that want to use

product or run a modified service without publishing changes.

Business / Enterprise features (federation across relays, SSO/SAML, audit logs,
admin console, managed hosting, advanced policies) are **not** part of this
AGPL repository; they are distributed separately under the commercial license.
See [`docs/COMMERCIAL.md`](docs/COMMERCIAL.md).
